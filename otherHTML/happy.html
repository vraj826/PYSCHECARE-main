Stay Happy

Happiness is not something ready-made. It comes from your own actions. Here are some simple ways to boost your mood and nurture a positive mindset every day.

1. Do Something You Love

Set aside time each day for an activity that brings you joy — whether it's painting, cooking, gaming, or just listening to music.

2. Connect with Others

Spend quality time with friends or family. Even a short conversation can lift your spirits significantly.

3. Practice Gratitude

Write down three things you're grateful for each day. This simple habit rewires your brain to notice the positive.

4. Move Your Body

Exercise releases endorphins — your brain's natural mood boosters. Even a 10-minute walk can make a difference.

5. Limit Social Media

Constant comparison on social media can quietly drain happiness. Set boundaries around screen time to protect your mental space.
